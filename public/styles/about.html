As of now I do programming only as a hobby just to solve my own problems. I have a few projects on my github account :

I blog on a variety of topics. To be frank with you I write to keep sane. I have discovered that writing is a good remedy for confusion and anxiety. It calms me down and makes me look at my problems more objectively.
